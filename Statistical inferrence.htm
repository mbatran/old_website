Background:

When any subscriber makes a phone activity (e.g. call, message or data connection) the mobile network operator store the approximate location along with the time of the activity for billing purposes. To monetize this data and convert it into business information, there is a need to know the demographic attributes for each subscriber. However, in developing countries personal information of subscribers are often unavailable or wrong since most people buy a SIM card from a third party vendor.

Variables are only Timestamp and Location for each subscriber and does not have any direct correlation to any of the label. First step is to Engineer some features that have a real world indication.

Daily routine is highly impacted by demographic attributes of subscribers. For example:

1) Subscriber who go to the same location between 8 am and 5 pm is most likely to have a stable job.

2) House wives tend to spend more time than working men

3) In Muslim countries, Males are supposed to pray every Friday at noon for a duration of 1 hour in an outdoor mosque, this means if we observe mobility every Friday at praying time for long period of time. There is a higher probability that this person is a Male.
